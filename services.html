AVAILABLE SERVICES

1.Cementitious Waterproofing

Cementitious Waterproofing is a type of protective coating created by mixing cement-based materials with special waterproofing agents.

2.Bituminous Waterproofing

Bituminous Waterproofing involves the use of bitumen-based materials to create a flexible, durable, and water-resistant barrier. These coatings are ideal for areas exposed to moisture, providing excellent protection and longevity for surfaces such as roofs, foundations, and basements.

3.Liquid Waterproofing Membranes

Liquid Waterproofing Membranes are an alternative to traditional sheet-based or torch-applied waterproofing systems. They can be either one-component (moisture curing) or two-component, where they cure as a result of mixing two components together.

4.Polyurethane Waterproofing

Polyurethane Waterproofing is an industrial-grade liquid-applied coating system that provides seamless protection and durability. It cures to form a flexible, UV-resistant membrane ideal for long-lasting waterproofing performance.

5. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) Waterproofing

EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) Waterproofing is a synthetic rubber membrane that can be applied over various surfaces to provide long-lasting, flexible, and weather-resistant waterproofing, ideal for roofs, terraces, and other exposed areas.

6.Torch-on Membranes

Torch-on Membranes are bitumen-based waterproofing sheets that are applied using heat, creating a durable, seamless barrier against water ingress, ideal for roofs and other exposed surfaces.

7.Crystalline Waterproofing

Crystalline Waterproofing is the process of using specially formulated compounds that penetrate concrete and form crystals within its pores and capillaries, effectively blocking water pathways and enhancing long-term water resistance from within the structure.

8.Integral Waterproofing

9.Water Repellent Coatings

Water Repellent Coatings are applied to the surface of materials like concrete, brick, or stone to create a protective barrier that repels water, helping to reduce moisture absorption while allowing the surface to breathe.

10.Sealants and Caulking

Sealants and Caulking are applied to joints, gaps, and seams in construction materials to block the passage of water, air, and other elements, ensuring a flexible, durable seal that accommodates movement.
